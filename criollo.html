  • Maíz Criollo decolonizes the corn and celebrates the variety of shapes and colors sustainable corn gives us. A sustainable and collective company that supports the local farmers that resist to keep the traditions alive. The identity has a restricted color palette taken from the colors of the non gmo corn. The logo is thought out to represent an abstraction of the corn in the pre-hispanic codex of Mexico
