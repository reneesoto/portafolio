  • AMBULANTE is a film festival tour that presents a collection of international films through out the country. This year's theme was the ecologies of the industry, therefore we decided to create a color palette that responds to the state of emergency the world is in; the black and orange also emulates the climate change and the rise of temperatures. The main typography changes with each format to represent the movement of the festival. We have a minimal design to frame the photography of the films projected.
